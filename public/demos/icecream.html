We've Been Scooping Since 2012

What started as a tiny cart at the Sugar City Farmers Market grew into the neighborhood's most beloved ice cream destination. We're a family-owned shop run by the Delgado family — Mom handles the flavors, Dad scoops, and the kids are the official taste-testers (tough job, someone's gotta do it).

Every batch is made fresh daily using locally sourced dairy, seasonal fruits, and flavor combinations dreamed up right here in our little kitchen. No shortcuts. Just pure, joyful scoops.
