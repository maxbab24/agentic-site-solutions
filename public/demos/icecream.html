We’ve Been Scooping Since 2012.

We’re a family-owned shop run by the Delgado family — Mom handles the flavors, Dad scoops, and the kids are the official taste-testers (tough job, someone’s gotta do it).

Every batch is made fresh daily using locally sourced dairy, seasonal fruits, and flavor combinations dreamed up right here in our little kitchen. No shortcuts. Just pure, joyful scoops.
